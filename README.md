# Optimize Your URLs with Slugify

Transform your URLs into clean, SEO-friendly versions automatically. Slugify integrates with your content management system, saving you time and enhancing your website's performance.

## Key Features

- **Automatic URL Generation**: Generate optimized URLs from your content titles without manual input.
- **CMS Integration**: Easily integrate Slugify with popular content management systems like WordPress and Drupal for instant URL optimization.
- **Customizable Slugs**: Modify generated slugs to fit your branding or specific requirements, ensuring consistency across your site.
- **Real-Time Preview**: View changes in real-time as you create or edit content, allowing for immediate adjustments.
